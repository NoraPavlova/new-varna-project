Please, get familiar with the rules below:
In your email, include a detailed description of the cause, including your ideas for improvement of the public space. Email can be written both in English or Bulgarian. Once we receive your suggestion, it will be reviewed, and we’ll get back to you. If it meets the requirements, stated below, the cause will be posted on the website.

After that moment, registered users can follow it and / or create events to support it.

Location is limited to Varna and Varna region.

Allowed initiatives: Cleaning public spaces and nature areas such as beaches and gardens; small repair jobs such as repairing broken benches, stairs or cleaning graffiti (the ugly kind) off buildings; improving the city environment and public spaces.

Forbidden initiatives: All types of collecting money or donations, cooking and distributing food to people or animals, all types of personal causes or funding. Suggestions of such kind won’t be approved.
